Chilli Potato

⏱️ 40 mins

Ingredients

  • 3 potatoes (cut into thin sticks)
  • 3 tbsp cornflour
  • 1 green chili (chopped)
  • ¼ tsp turmeric
  • Fresh coriander – for garnish
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ cup oil
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp tomato ketchup

Instructions

1. Boil potato sticks for 2–3 mins. Drain, coat with cornflour, and deep-fry until crispy.

2. In a pan, sauté garlic and green chili. Add sauces, salt, pepper, and a splash of water.

3. Add fried potatoes, mix well to coat. Garnish with spring onions and serve hot!
