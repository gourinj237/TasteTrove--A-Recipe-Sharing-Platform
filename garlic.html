Garlic Cheese Bread

⏱️ 20 mins

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread or 1 small baguette (halved)
  • 2 tbsp butter (softened)
  • 1–2 tsp garlic (minced or grated)
  • ½ cup grated cheese (mozzarella or mixed)
  • 1 tsp dried oregano or mixed herbs
  • Chopped coriander or parsley (optional)

Instructions

1. Mix softened butter with garlic and herbs.

2. Spread garlic butter over bread slices. Sprinkle cheese generously on top.

3. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 8–10 mins until cheese melts and edges are crispy.

4. Garnish with parsley/coriander and enjoy with ketchup or dip!
