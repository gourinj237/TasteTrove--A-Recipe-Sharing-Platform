Avocado Toast

⏱️ 10 min

A healthy and satisfying toast topped with creamy avocado, fresh herbs, and a touch of lemon — perfect for your morning boost!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of whole grain bread
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Chili flakes (optional)
  • Fresh lemon juice

Instructions

1. Toast the bread slices until golden.

2. Mash the avocado in a bowl and mix with salt, pepper, and lemon juice.

3. Spread the mixture onto the toasted bread.

4. Top with chili flakes or herbs if desired. Serve immediately.
