Mango Smoothie

⏱️ 15 mins

Ingredients

  • 1 large ripe mango (peeled & chopped)
  • ½ cup yogurt or curd
  • ½ cup chilled milk (or water for lighter version)
  • 1–2 tsp honey or sugar (optional, adjust to taste)
  • A few ice cubes (optional)
  • A pinch of cardamom (optional, for flavor)

Instructions

1. Add mango, yogurt, milk, sweetener, and ice to a blender.

2. Blend until creamy and lump-free.

3. Pour into glasses. Garnish with mango cubes or mint (optional). Enjoy cold!
